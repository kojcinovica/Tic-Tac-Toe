**Tic Tac Toe – v1**

**Overview**

A simple Tic Tac Toe game built in Unity. Play against another player locally and enjoy a clean, minimal interface with classic gameplay.

**Features**
- 2-player local gameplay (Player X vs Player O)
- Simple and intuitive UI
- Win/draw detection
- Lightweight and beginner-friendly project structure
  
**Controls**
Mouse: Click or tap on a grid cell to place your mark (X or O)

**How to Play**
- The game is played on a 3×3 grid.
- Players take turns placing their marks (X or O).
- The first player to align three marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.
- If all cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.
